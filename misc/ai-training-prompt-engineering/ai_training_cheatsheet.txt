SLIDE 4 — DEMO 1 — WEAK vs STRONG
---------------------------------
Weak (text):
  Explain IBM App Connect Enterprise.

Strong (text):
  Explain IBM App Connect Enterprise to a business manager
  in 3 sentences. No jargon. Focus on business value.

Weak (multimodal — Gemini/Veo3):
  Make a video where theres like a box from ikea and it
  just like explodes and all the furniture flies out.

Strong (multimodal — Gemini/Veo3 structured):
  {
    "description": "Cinematic shot of a sunlit Scandinavian bedroom...",
    "style": "cinematic",
    "camera": "fixed wide angle",
    "lighting": "natural warm with cool accents",
    "room": "Scandinavian bedroom",
    "elements": [ "IKEA box (logo visible)", "bed with yellow throw", ... ],
    "motion": "box opens, furniture assembles rapidly",
    "ending": "calm, modern space with yellow IKEA accent",
    "text": "none",
    "keywords": ["16:9","Scandinavian","no text","warm & cool tones"]
  }

Say:
  "Better instructions = better output, especially in multimodal."

  Rewrite the text below in the same style as examples A & B.

  main takeaway on improving prompt instructions?
